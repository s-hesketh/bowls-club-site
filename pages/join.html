Come and Join Us!

Crown Green Bowls is a game for all ages, easy to play but takes a lot longer to master! It's an inexpensive sport, an ideal way of making new friends and helps to keep you fit. Etwall is a friendly club with an excellent bowling green and always extends a warm welcome to new members.

The club runs numerous men, women and mixed teams in several leagues catering for all standards from beginner to experienced. You can join as a social member which entitles you to use the facilities and play on the green whenever there is not a match being played, or join as a full member and play in one of our many leagues teams.

We have several teams which are perfect for members new to the sport, where you can enjoy competitive bowls in a relaxed atmosphere as your skill and experience builds. Take a look at our Teams page for more details.

The bowling season normally lasts from early April to end of September. If interested come and give it a try without obligation. All you need to begin with is a flat-soled pair of shoes and a willingness to try something different!
