How to find us

The green is situated next to King George Playing Field in Etwall. The address is Portland Street, Etwall, Derbyshire DE65 6JF.

Vehicular access to the green is via Willington Road and then Portland Street, which is to the rear of the Spread Eagle Public House. The street is narrow, and the car park is straight up at the top of the street. There is a slight incline at the entrance to the car park.
